Performance Summary

Our models are currently underperforming with only 53.5% accuracy. This is barely better than a coin flip (50%).

Why is performance low?

  • Limited historical data (only 2 years)
  • Missing key features (COT data, options flow)
  • Basic models not tuned for time series
